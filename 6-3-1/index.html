    The table below contains more information regarding the indicator.

    Indicator name

    Water use growth rate
    Target

    Promote the conservation and wise use of water to achieve a 30% reduction or increased efficiency in water use in various sectors by 2025 (based on 2009 water use levels)
    Indicator description

    This indicator measure the growth rate in water use in different sector of the economy as well as by households.
    Geographical coverage

    Canada
    Unit of measurement

    Percentage
    Comments and limitations

    The estimate for water use does not include the use of water for hydro-electricity production.
